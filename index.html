Hello, I'm
Ravi!

A Sushegaad Goenkar!

I grew up in Divar, Goa — also called Devwadi — surrounded by nature, quiet lanes, and the sea. Not the Goa you see in movies with crowds and chaos, but a serene, peaceful place closer to nature. Those early years taught me to enjoy life's simple moments: watching sunsets on the beach, getting wet in the rain, and finding peace in the small things around me.

Today, I live in Bengaluru and work as a Network Engineer. My job has shaped me into someone who stays calm, thinks clearly, and solves problems with patience and intention. It has also strengthened my values of discipline, focus, and steady growth.

I'm an introvert who prefers minimalism and quiet spaces. I enjoy long walks, cooking something simple, riding my motorcycle, or listening to music after a long day. Nutrition, home workouts, and meditation keep me physically, mentally, and spiritually balanced. These small routines keep me grounded.

I believe in steady self-improvement—whether it's through learning, building discipline, or simply being more aware of how I live each day. I'm currently working towards advancing my career while also exploring opportunities abroad, with Ireland being a place that interests me for the next chapter of my journey.

Lately, I've been exploring philosophy and find myself drawn to the works of Franz Kafka—his thoughts on existence, solitude, and the human condition resonate deeply with me and often stay with me long after I've finished reading.

I live a clean and mindful lifestyle. I haven't consumed sugar for the past 18 months and consciously stay away from smoking, alcohol, drugs, and aerated drinks. For me, discipline is not restriction—it's a way to stay clear, present, and in control of my life.

I also value family deeply and share a close bond with my sisters. Relationships, for me, are built on understanding, respect, and emotional maturity rather than just words.

This space is where I share what matters to me: balance, simplicity, and the journey of becoming a better version of myself. I value meaningful connections, quiet understanding, and a sense of companionship that feels effortless. Dev bare korum.

If something here connects with you, feel free to reach out.
