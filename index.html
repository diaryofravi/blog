Hello, I'm
Ravi!

A Sushegaad Goenkar!

I'm a Goan, originally from Divar (Devwadi), where I grew up around quiet surroundings, nature, and a slower pace of life. That environment shaped how I see things—I tend to value simplicity, calm, and the small, everyday moments.

I currently live in Bengaluru and work as a Network Engineer. My work has made me more patient, structured, and focused on steady progress rather than quick results.

I prefer a simple and fairly routine lifestyle. Most of my time outside work goes into staying active, cooking basic meals, and keeping things balanced through workouts and meditation. I'm not very active on social media and generally prefer quieter ways of spending time.

I'm someone who is comfortable being on my own, but I also value meaningful connections. I believe relationships work best when there is mutual respect, understanding, and emotional stability.

At this stage, I'm focused on growing in my career and am open to opportunities abroad, particularly in Scandinavian countries. Over the long term, I see myself moving towards a more grounded and simple way of living.

This space is just a reflection of how I think and live. If you'd like to understand me beyond a profile, you can explore the blog.
